---
title: "Brand Kit: Apply Your Brand Identity to Every Creative"
description: "Centralize your brand colors, fonts, and logo in one place — CreatAds applies your Brand Kit automatically to every creative you generate."
---

Brand Kit is where you store your brand's visual identity. Once configured, CreatAds applies it automatically every time you generate a creative — no need to re-enter colors or re-upload logos for each campaign. Each workspace has its own Brand Kit, so you can manage multiple brands or clients without mixing their identities.

## What Brand Kit contains

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| **Brand name** | Displayed on visuals and used in AI-generated copy |
| **Description** | Short context to guide the tone and style of generated text |
| **Primary color** | The dominant brand color (default: black) |
| **Secondary color** | Accent color (default: violet) |
| **Accent color** | Details and highlights (default: lime green) |
| **Primary font** | Headline typeface (default: Geist) |
| **Secondary font** | Body typeface (default: Inter) |
| **Logo** | Light version and dark version (URL or file upload) |

## Configuring your Brand Kit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Brand Setup">
    In the sidebar, click **Brand Setup** (under the Audiences section).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your brand name and description">
    Fill in the brand name and a short description. The description shapes the tone of all AI-generated text across your creatives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your colors">
    Enter each color as a hex code (`#RRGGBB`). Define your primary, secondary, and accent colors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Upload a light-background version and a dark-background version of your logo if available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The richer and more specific your brand description, the more consistent the tone of AI-generated text will be across every creative. Include your positioning, audience, and key differentiators.
</Tip>

## Activating Brand Kit on a generation

**AI Studio** — if a Brand Kit is configured for the active workspace, it is applied automatically. You will see it confirmed below the generation form (for example, `Acme · active`). No extra step required.

**Campaigns** — in Step 2 of the campaign wizard, enable the **Use Brand Kit** toggle to apply your brand identity to all creatives in the batch.

## Importing from a document

If your brand identity is already defined in a brief, a PDF, or a website, you can extract it automatically rather than filling in the form by hand.

Click **Import from document** in Brand Setup, then paste a URL or upload a PDF file. The AI extracts the brand name, description, colors, and positioning in seconds and pre-fills the form for you to review.

<Warning>
  Each workspace has its own Brand Kit. If you manage multiple brands or clients, make sure you are in the correct workspace before editing Brand Kit settings. Saving changes in the wrong workspace will overwrite that client's brand identity.
</Warning>
