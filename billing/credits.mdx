Credits are the unit of consumption in CreatAds. Every image you generate — whether in the Studio or as part of a campaign — costs exactly one credit. When you reach your plan's monthly limit, generation stops until your credits reset or you upgrade.

## What counts as a credit

One credit equals one image generation. This applies consistently across the platform:

- Generating an image in the AI Studio costs 1 credit.
- Running a campaign with a volume of 12 produces 12 images and consumes 12 credits.

There are no partial credits. Each completed generation deducts one credit from your balance, regardless of the image format, size, or campaign settings.

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance is always visible at the bottom of the sidebar in the **Credits** block. It shows:

- Your active plan name
- A progress bar reflecting consumption
- Your exact credit count (used / total)
- The percentage of credits remaining

```text
Credits                          Studio
████████░░  8 / 60       33% remaining
```

## The 80% alert

When you have consumed 80% or more of your monthly credits, a red blinking dot appears next to the Credits block. This alert is a signal to review your usage and consider upgrading before generation stops.

<Note>
  Credits are shared across all surfaces where you use CreatAds — including the web app and the developer platform (API, CLI, and SDK). Usage from any of these counts against the same monthly balance.
</Note>

## Monthly reset

Credits reset automatically at the start of each billing cycle. They do not carry over from one month to the next. If you have unused credits at the end of a billing period, they expire when the new cycle begins.
