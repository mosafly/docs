CreatAds is an AI-powered platform that generates Meta ad creatives — Facebook and Instagram visuals — in minutes. You describe your brand, define your audience profiles, and CreatAds produces campaign-ready images in every format you need: feed, portrait, stories, and landscape. Whether you use the web app, the CLI, the TypeScript SDK, or an AI agent via MCP, the same generation pipeline is at your fingertips.

## How CreatAds works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your brand workspace">
    Create a client workspace for your brand or agency client, then configure your Brand Kit with colors, logo, and brand description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate audience profiles">
    Let the AI create up to 10 audience personas from your brand description — each with a persona, pain point, and messaging angle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Choose your audience profiles, ad formats, CTA, offer text, and volume. CreatAds distributes creatives across every profile and format combination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or publish">
    Export creatives as high-resolution PNGs, download a ZIP, or publish directly to Meta Ads.
  </Step>
</Steps>
