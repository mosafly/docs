CreatAds offers four plans designed for different levels of creative output — from free discovery to unlimited high-volume production. All plans give you access to the core generation features, but premium plans unlock Brand Setup, direct Meta Ads publishing, and API access.

## Plans at a glance

| Plan | Generations / month | Price | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Solo** | 2 | Free | Discovery, testing |
| **Studio** | 60 | €49/month | Freelancers, small brands |
| **Agence** | 250 | €149/month | Agencies, teams |
| **Scale** | Unlimited | €299/month | High-volume production |

## Free plan limitations

The Solo plan lets you explore CreatAds at no cost, but it is limited to 2 generations per month and does not include:

- **Brand Setup** and audience profile generation
- **Meta Ads direct publishing**

<Note>
  Upgrading to Studio or higher unlocks Brand Setup and direct Meta Ads publishing. These features are not available on the free Solo plan.
</Note>

## How to upgrade

You can upgrade your plan in two ways:

- Click **Upgrade to Studio** at the bottom of the sidebar.
- Go to **Settings → Subscription** and select a new plan.

Plan changes take effect immediately. The credits from your new plan are added to your current billing cycle right away — you do not need to wait until the next renewal date.

## Managing your subscription

Go to **Settings → Subscription** to manage everything related to your plan:

- View your current plan and next renewal date
- Upgrade or downgrade to a different plan
- Access your invoices and billing history
- Cancel your subscription

## Developer platform access

The developer platform — including the API, CLI, and SDK — requires an active premium subscription (Studio or higher). The free Solo plan does not include developer platform access.
