CreatAds is an AI-powered platform that generates Meta ad creatives — Facebook and Instagram visuals — in minutes. You describe your brand, configure your Brand Kit, and let the AI produce campaign-ready images across every format you need. Whether you are a solo marketer, an agency managing multiple clients, or a developer integrating creative generation into a product, CreatAds handles the heavy lifting.

## What you can do with CreatAds

- **AI Studio** — generate a single ad visual on demand without setting up a campaign. Ideal for quick tests and one-off posts.
- **Campaigns** — batch-generate multiple creatives across formats and audience profiles in one operation.
- **Audience Profiles** — automatically create up to 10 AI-powered personas from your brand description, each with a distinct messaging angle.
- **Brand Kit** — store your colors, logo, and brand description so every generated visual stays on-brand.

## Supported ad formats

| Format | Placement |
|--------|-----------|
| `1:1` | Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed |
| `4:5` | Instagram Feed portrait (highest engagement) |
| `9:16` | Stories & Reels |
| `1.91:1` | Facebook Landscape / Link ads |

## Who CreatAds is for

CreatAds is built for marketers, agencies, and brands who need volume and consistency without a design team, as well as developers who want to embed creative generation directly into their workflows via the CLI, SDK, or MCP server.
