AI Studio lets you generate a single ad visual in under two minutes. There is no campaign to configure and no audience profile to select — just a brief, an optional image, a format, and one click. It is the fastest way to test a creative idea or produce a one-off visual for a post or ad.

## Studio vs. Campaigns

Use **AI Studio** when you want to generate one visual quickly, iterate on a brief, or try a new creative direction without committing to a full batch run.

Use **Campaigns** when you need multiple creatives across audience profiles and formats at once. Campaigns distribute volume across profiles and formats automatically — see [Campaigns](/features/campaigns) for details.

## Generating a visual

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your images (optional)">
    Upload a **reference image** — an existing ad, brand photo, or mood board — to guide the AI's style and composition. You can also upload a **product image** to place your product in the scene. Both inputs accept PNG or JPG files. You can import from your computer or choose from your library.

    If you have a Brand Kit configured, its colors and visual identity are applied automatically. You will see it displayed below the form (for example, `Acme · active`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your brief">
    Describe the visual you want in the text field (500 characters max). Be specific about atmosphere, colors, props, and the message you want to communicate.

    ```text
    Parisian café atmosphere, golden light. Dark chocolate packshot on a marble
    table. Text: "Free delivery over $35."
    ```

    Use the AI suggestion buttons below the field to enrich your brief if you need inspiration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a format">
    Select the format that matches your placement:

    | Format | Dimensions | Placement |
    |---|---|---|
    | Square `1:1` | 1080 × 1080 px | Facebook & Instagram feed |
    | Story `9:16` | 1080 × 1920 px | Stories & Reels |
    | Landscape `1.91:1` | 1200 × 628 px | Facebook link ads |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. A progress counter shows the generation steps in real time. Your visual is ready in **30–90 seconds**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After generation

Once your visual is ready, you have three options:

- **Download** — export a high-resolution PNG file.
- **Publish to Meta** — send the creative directly to Meta Ads (requires a connected Meta account).
- **Iterate** — edit your brief and regenerate to refine the result.
