---
title: "Get Started with CreatAds: Generate Your First Ad in Minutes"
description: "Create your account, configure your brand workspace, and generate your first on-brand Meta ad creative with CreatAds in a few simple steps."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank account to a finished, campaign-ready Meta ad creative. Follow the steps below in order for the best results.

<Note>
  The free Solo plan includes 2 generations so you can try the platform without a credit card. It does not include Brand Setup (colors and logo configuration).
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [creatads.app](https://creatads.app) and click **Get started for free**. The free plan gives you 2 generations to test the platform — no credit card required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first client workspace">
    The onboarding assistant guides you through creating a **client workspace** — an isolated space for a single brand or advertiser. Give the workspace the name of your brand or client (for example, "Nike France" or "Maison Léon").

    You can create as many workspaces as you need. Each workspace has its own campaigns, audience profiles, and Brand Kit. To switch between workspaces, use the selector at the top of the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your Brand Kit">
    Open **Brand Setup** in the Audiences section of the sidebar. Add your brand's primary colors, upload your logo, and write a short brand description. A well-configured Brand Kit makes every generated visual more accurate and consistent.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate audience profiles">
    Go to **Profiles** and click **Generate profiles**. CreatAds reads your brand description and creates up to 10 AI personas — each with a distinct audience segment, pain point, and messaging angle. Generation takes approximately 30–60 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Open **Campaigns** and click **New campaign**. Select the audience profiles you want to target, choose your ad formats (1:1, 4:5, 9:16, or 1.91:1), set your CTA and offer text, and choose how many creatives to generate per profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download or publish your creatives">
    Once generation is complete, you can download individual creatives as high-resolution PNGs, export a ZIP archive, or publish directly to Meta Ads from the campaign results view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For the best results, always work in this order: **Brand Kit** first, then **Audience Profiles**, then **Campaign**. Setting up your brand before generating profiles ensures the AI personas reflect your actual brand positioning, which produces more relevant and on-brand creatives.
</Tip>
